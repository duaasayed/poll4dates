Welcome to poll4dates

Connect anywhere around the globe. Coordinate across timezones with Poll4Dates.

Plan Event

Let us show you how

Simply create a poll with any number of date and time options for your event. Then send the poll to your guests either by sharing the general link to the poll or by having Poll4Dates send them an email invitation to vote in the poll. That’s it! You can watch the votes come in real-time as your guests enter their preferences. When ready, pick the best date/time for the event directly from the poll. Check out our Help Center for more details.

Select date/time options for your event and share with your friends.

Without having to log in, your friends can mark their availability for the proposed dates.

Schedule your event on the date that works for everyone!
